TYLER THE CREATOR

Tyler Gregory Okonma (born March 6, 1991), known professionally as Tyler, the Creator, is an American rapper, singer-songwriter, and record producer.He has been cited as an influential figure in alternative hip-hop during the 2010s. In 2024, the Los Angeles Times featured Okonma in its "L.A. Influential" series as a "creator who is leaving their mark" in Los Angeles.
